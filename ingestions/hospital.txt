HOSPITAL OPERATIONS (DETAILED WORKFLOWS)

1. OPD CONSULTATION PROCESS:
- Patient arrives at reception
- Registration is completed
- Token number is generated
- Patient waits in OPD area
- Doctor consultation happens
- Prescription is given
- Patient proceeds to pharmacy or diagnostics if required

----------------------------------------

2. APPOINTMENT BOOKING PROCESS:
- Appointment can be booked via:
  - Reception
  - Phone call
  - Email
- Patient selects doctor and time slot
- Appointment is confirmed based on availability
- Booking ID is generated
- Patient must report 15 minutes before time

----------------------------------------

3. EMERGENCY HANDLING PROCESS:
- Patient is brought to emergency ward
- Immediate triage is performed
- Vital signs are checked
- Emergency doctor provides initial treatment
- If critical → shifted to ICU
- If stable → moved to appropriate department

----------------------------------------

4. ADMISSION PROCESS (IPD):
- Doctor recommends admission
- Patient registration is verified
- Admission form is filled
- Room type is selected (General / Private / ICU)
- Bed is allocated
- Nursing staff initiates monitoring

----------------------------------------

5. DISCHARGE PROCESS:
- Doctor approves discharge
- Final reports are prepared
- Billing department generates invoice
- Payment is completed
- Discharge summary is handed to patient

----------------------------------------

6. SURGERY PROCESS:
- Doctor evaluates need for surgery
- Pre-operative tests are conducted
- Patient consent is taken
- Operation theatre is scheduled
- Surgery is performed
- Post-operative monitoring in recovery room
- Shift to ward or ICU

----------------------------------------

7. ICU MANAGEMENT PROCESS:
- Continuous monitoring of vital signs
- Specialized nursing care
- Restricted visitor access
- Regular doctor rounds
- Emergency response readiness

----------------------------------------

8. DIAGNOSTIC PROCESS:
- Doctor prescribes tests
- Patient visits lab/radiology
- Sample collection or scan performed
- Reports generated
- Reports shared with doctor

----------------------------------------

9. PHARMACY PROCESS:
- Prescription is submitted
- Medicines are verified
- Billing is generated
- Medicines are dispensed

----------------------------------------

10. BILLING PROCESS:
- All services are recorded
- Final bill is generated
- Insurance (if applicable) is processed
- Payment is completed at billing counter

----------------------------------------

COMMON CONDITIONS → DEPARTMENT MAPPING:

- Bone injury → Orthopedics
