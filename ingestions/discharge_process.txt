----------------------------------------
PATIENT DISCHARGE PROCESS
----------------------------------------

Discharge is the process of releasing a patient from the hospital after treatment is completed or when the patient is stable.

----------------------------------------
WHEN DISCHARGE HAPPENS
----------------------------------------
- Treatment is completed
- Patient condition is stable
- Doctor approves discharge
- Patient requests discharge (with doctor consent)

----------------------------------------
STEP-BY-STEP DISCHARGE FLOW
----------------------------------------

STEP 1: DOCTOR APPROVAL
- Treating doctor evaluates patient condition
- Confirms patient is fit for discharge
- Writes discharge summary

STEP 2: DISCHARGE SUMMARY PREPARATION
- Diagnosis details
- Treatment given
- Medications prescribed
- Follow-up instructions
- Lifestyle recommendations

STEP 3: FINAL BILLING
- All services are calculated:
  - Room charges
  - Doctor consultation
  - Tests & diagnostics
  - Medications
  - Procedures or surgeries
- Final bill is generated

STEP 4: PAYMENT CLEARANCE
- Patient or attendant clears pending amount
- Insurance processing (if applicable)
- Receipt is generated

STEP 5: MEDICATION & INSTRUCTIONS
- Pharmacy provides prescribed medicines
- Nurse explains:
  - Medication schedule
  - Diet instructions
  - Activity restrictions

STEP 6: DISCHARGE EXECUTION
- Patient leaves hospital
- Follow-up visit is scheduled if needed

----------------------------------------
EMERGENCY DISCHARGE (LAMA / DAMA)
----------------------------------------
- LAMA: Leave Against Medical Advice
- DAMA: Discharge Against Medical Advice

Conditions:
- Patient chooses to leave early
- Must sign consent form
- Hospital is not responsible after discharge

----------------------------------------
FOLLOW-UP CARE
----------------------------------------
- Patient may need revisit
- Follow-up date is provided
- Reports should be carried

----------------------------------------
IMPORTANT RULES
----------------------------------------
- Doctor approval is mandatory
- Bills must be cleared before leaving
- Discharge summary is compulsory
- Follow-up instructions must be followed
