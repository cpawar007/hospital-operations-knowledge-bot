FIRST AID GUIDE - ABC MULTISPECIALTY HOSPITAL

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS:
- Stay calm and ensure the scene is safe.
- Check if the person is conscious and breathing.
- Call for medical help if the condition is serious.
- Do not give food or drink unless necessary.
- Always seek professional medical help after first aid.

----------------------------------------

FEVER:
- Give plenty of fluids (water, ORS).
- Use a cold compress on forehead.
- Rest in a cool environment.
- If temperature is very high or persists, consult a doctor.

----------------------------------------

HEADACHE:
- Rest in a quiet, dark room.
- Drink water to avoid dehydration.
- Apply a cold or warm compress on head/neck.
- If severe or frequent, consult a doctor.

----------------------------------------

DIZZINESS:
- Make the person sit or lie down immediately.
- Elevate legs slightly if possible.
- Give water slowly.
- Avoid sudden movements.

----------------------------------------

COUGH / SORE THROAT:
- Drink warm fluids.
- Gargle with warm salt water.
- Avoid cold drinks.
- If persistent, consult a doctor.

----------------------------------------

CHEST PAIN:
- Make the person sit and stay calm.
- Loosen tight clothing.
- Call emergency services immediately.
- Do NOT ignore chest pain.

----------------------------------------

BURNS:
- Cool the burn under running water for 10-15 minutes.
- Do NOT apply ice directly.
- Cover with clean cloth or sterile dressing.
- Do NOT burst blisters.

----------------------------------------

CUTS / BLEEDING:
- Apply direct pressure with clean cloth.
- Elevate injured area if possible.
- Clean wound with clean water.
- Cover with bandage.

----------------------------------------

FRACTURE (BROKEN BONE):
- Keep injured area still.
- Do NOT try to realign bone.
- Use a splint if available.
- Seek immediate medical help.

----------------------------------------

VOMITING:
- Keep person hydrated with small sips of water.
- Avoid solid food for some time.
- Rest properly.
- If severe or continuous, consult doctor.

----------------------------------------

DIARRHEA:
- Drink ORS frequently.
- Avoid oily/spicy food.
- Maintain hygiene.
- Seek help if dehydration occurs.

----------------------------------------

BREATHING DIFFICULTY:
- Help person sit upright.
- Loosen tight clothes.
- Ensure fresh air.
- Seek immediate medical attention.

----------------------------------------

IMPORTANT:
- First aid is temporary support, not a replacement for medical treatment.
- Always follow up with a qualified doctor.

END OF GUIDE
