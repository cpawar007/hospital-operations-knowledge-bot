----------------------------------------
HOSPITAL POLICIES & RULES
----------------------------------------

This document defines operational rules, patient policies, and hospital guidelines for Healing Touch Hospital.

----------------------------------------
1. GENERAL PATIENT RULES
----------------------------------------
- All patients must register at reception before consultation
- Valid ID proof is required for registration
- OPD tokens are mandatory for consultation
- Patients must follow assigned appointment timings
- Emergency cases are prioritized over scheduled patients

----------------------------------------
2. VISITING RULES (INPATIENT)
----------------------------------------
- Visiting hours:
  - 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM (General Ward)
  - ICU: Restricted (Only 10–15 minutes with permission)

- Maximum 2 visitors per patient
- Children under 12 not allowed in ICU
- Silence must be maintained in wards
- No food delivery to ICU patients

----------------------------------------
3. EMERGENCY RULES
----------------------------------------
- No appointment required for emergency cases
- Patients are treated immediately based on severity
- Billing is secondary in life-threatening cases
- Ambulance service available 24/7
- Family consent may be bypassed in critical cases

----------------------------------------
4. PAYMENT & BILLING RULES
----------------------------------------
- OPD consultation fees must be paid before visit
- IPD charges are calculated daily
- Surgery and ICU charges are separate
- Insurance claims accepted (subject to approval)
- Final bill is generated at discharge

----------------------------------------
5. APPOINTMENT RULES
----------------------------------------
- Appointments must be booked in advance
- Late arrival beyond 15 minutes may lead to cancellation
- Doctor availability is fixed per schedule
- Rescheduling allowed once per appointment

----------------------------------------
6. MEDICATION RULES
----------------------------------------
- Medicines must be taken only as prescribed
- Antibiotics cannot be sold without prescription
- Pharmacy cannot change prescribed drugs
- Dosage instructions must be strictly followed

----------------------------------------
7. DIAGNOSTIC RULES
----------------------------------------
- Tests require doctor recommendation
- Reports are usually available within:
  - Blood test: 6–12 hours
  - Imaging: 24–48 hours
- Reports must be reviewed by doctor before treatment changes

----------------------------------------
8. ADMISSION RULES
----------------------------------------
- Admission requires doctor recommendation
- Bed allocation depends on availability and severity
- Private rooms available at extra cost
- Patient transfer between departments is allowed only with approval

----------------------------------------
9. ICU RULES
----------------------------------------
- ICU admission only for critical patients
- Continuous monitoring is mandatory
- Mobile phones not allowed inside ICU
- Entry restricted to authorized staff only
- Family updates provided periodically by doctor

----------------------------------------
10. DISCHARGE RULES
----------------------------------------
- Patient discharged only after doctor approval
- Discharge summary is mandatory
- Follow-up appointment may be scheduled
- Pending bills must be cleared before exit

----------------------------------------
11. EMERGENCY EXCEPTIONS
----------------------------------------
- Rules may be bypassed in life-threatening situations
- Doctor has final authority in emergency decisions
- Consent may be waived in critical care cases
