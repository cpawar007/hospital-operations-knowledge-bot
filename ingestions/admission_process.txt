----------------------------------------
PATIENT ADMISSION PROCESS (IPD)
----------------------------------------

Admission is required when a patient needs continuous monitoring, advanced treatment, or surgery.

----------------------------------------
WHEN ADMISSION IS REQUIRED
----------------------------------------
- Severe illness or condition
- Doctor recommendation after consultation
- Emergency cases
- Surgery or procedure requirement
- Observation for unstable patients

----------------------------------------
ADMISSION TYPES
----------------------------------------
1. Planned Admission (Scheduled)
2. Emergency Admission (Immediate)

----------------------------------------
STEP-BY-STEP ADMISSION FLOW
----------------------------------------

STEP 1: DOCTOR EVALUATION
- Patient is examined by a doctor
- Doctor decides if admission is required
- Admission note is created

STEP 2: REGISTRATION
- Patient details are recorded:
  - Name
  - Age
  - Gender
  - Contact details
  - ID proof
- Unique Patient ID is generated

STEP 3: BED ALLOCATION
- Room type is selected:
  - General Ward
  - Semi-Private Room
  - Private Room
  - ICU (for critical patients)
- Bed is assigned based on availability

STEP 4: INITIAL ASSESSMENT
- Vital signs recorded:
  - Blood pressure
  - Heart rate
  - Temperature
- Nurse prepares patient file

STEP 5: PAYMENT / DEPOSIT
- Initial deposit is required
- Billing account is created
- Insurance details (if applicable) are recorded

STEP 6: SHIFT TO ROOM
- Patient is transferred to assigned bed/room
- Nursing staff begins monitoring

STEP 7: TREATMENT STARTS
- Doctor prescribes treatment plan
- Medications and tests are initiated

----------------------------------------
ICU ADMISSION (SPECIAL CASE)
----------------------------------------
- Direct admission without waiting
- Continuous monitoring
- Life-support systems if needed
- Restricted visitor access

----------------------------------------
IMPORTANT RULES
----------------------------------------
- Admission requires doctor approval (except emergency)
- Emergency cases bypass normal waiting
- ICU is prioritized for critical patients
- Payment must be initiated before admission (except emergency)

----------------------------------------
DISCHARGE CONDITION
----------------------------------------
Patient can be discharged when:
- Stable condition
- Treatment completed
- Doctor approval given
