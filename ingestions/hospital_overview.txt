Healing Touch Multispecialty Hospital

----------------------------------------
ABOUT HOSPITAL
----------------------------------------
Healing Touch Multispecialty Hospital is a full-service healthcare institution providing comprehensive medical care including outpatient (OPD), inpatient (IPD), emergency, surgical, and critical care services.

The hospital is designed to handle both routine and complex medical cases with a focus on patient safety, efficient operations, and quality treatment.

----------------------------------------
CORE SERVICES
----------------------------------------
- Outpatient Consultation (OPD)
- Inpatient Admission (IPD)
- Emergency & Trauma Care (24/7)
- Surgical Services (Minor & Major)
- ICU & Critical Care
- Diagnostic Services (Lab & Radiology)
- Pharmacy Services
- Ambulance & Patient Transport

----------------------------------------
GENERAL PATIENT FLOW (HIGH LEVEL)
----------------------------------------
1. Patient arrives at hospital
2. Registration at reception
3. Doctor consultation
4. Diagnostic tests (if required)
5. Treatment / Medication / Admission
6. Discharge & follow-up

----------------------------------------
IMPORTANT RULES
----------------------------------------
- Emergency cases are prioritized over OPD
- Critical patients are directly shifted to ICU
- Doctor allocation is based on department and condition
- Admission requires registration and doctor approval
- Surgery requires pre-operative evaluation
